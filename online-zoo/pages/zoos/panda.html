DID YOU KNOW?

Giant pandas are bears that are native to south-central China. They are easily recognized by the large, distinctive black patches around their eyes, over the ears, and across their round bodies.

  • Common name: Giant Panda
  • Scientific name: Ailuropoda melanoleuca
  • Type: Mammals
  • Diet: Herbivore
